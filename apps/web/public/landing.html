Platform Architecture

Two systems.
One unstoppable platform.

The hardest architectural decision BOSS makes — and enforces — is the absolute separation of intelligence and execution.

The Brain
MCP — Master Control Platform
Intelligence Layer
Owns all knowledge. Provides all recommendations. Never executes a single workflow.
  • Knowledge graph & business ontology
  • KPI library & constraint registry
  • Prompt, policy & workflow registries
  • Industry models (dental, legal, home services…)
  • AI employee definitions & memory
  • Recommendations & decision support
The Engine
Loop Runtime
Execution Layer
Executes everything. Manages state, retries, approvals. Contains zero business knowledge.
  • Workflow & decision engines
  • Scheduling & approval engines
  • Notification & recovery engines
  • Immutable audit log
  • Telemetry & observability
  • Dead letter queues & compensation
