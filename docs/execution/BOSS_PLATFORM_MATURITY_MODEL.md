# BOSS Platform Maturity Model

## Authority

Adopted 2026-06-28 as the roadmap classification model. Adoption does not imply
that every listed capability is implemented or certified.

| Level | Purpose | Capabilities | Gate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | Core Platform | Identity, tenancy, registries, events, Execution Backbone, Business Context, Knowledge Platform, CPP, UCR | Frozen certified platform contracts |
| 1 | Capability Platform | SDK, governance, marketplace, lifecycle, versioning, compatibility | Packs complete full lifecycle |
| 2 | Business Intelligence | Diagnostics, health, gaps | Evidence-backed observations only |
| 3 | Business Strategy | Reasoning, objectives, constraints | Certified measurable strategy |
| 4 | Business Planning | Planning, priority, resources | Executable work model |
| 5 | Business Execution | Workflow, automation, AI, policy, approvals | Auditable execution |
| 6 | Optimization | Optimization, forecast, simulation, recommendations | Governed predictive outputs |
| 7 | Executive Operations | Mission Control, dashboard, ledger, operating cycle | Continuous governed operations |

Every level requires registry/runtime/event integration, security, tenant
isolation, observability, performance, documentation, and certification. A
higher level may consume only certified lower-level contracts.
