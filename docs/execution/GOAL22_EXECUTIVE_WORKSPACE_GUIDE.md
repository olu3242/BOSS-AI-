# BOSS Executive Workspace — User Guide

**Date:** 2026-07-01
**Version:** 1.0

---

## What Is the Executive Workspace?

The Executive Workspace is the single place where a business owner can understand the full state of their business and take action — in under 60 seconds.

It surfaces every capability built across Goals 1–22 through a unified navigation shell. Nothing is hidden behind separate apps, separate logins, or separate dashboards.

---

## Getting There

```
/business/[businessId]/workspace
```

After completing the Business MRI, every business owner lands here by default.

---

## Navigation

The workspace has six sections accessible from the top navigation bar:

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Overview** | Your business at a glance |
| **Timeline** | Every event that happened, in order |
| **Approvals** | Decisions and recommendations awaiting your action |
| **Automation** | Connected tools and what they've done |
| **Intelligence** | Root causes, KPIs, and the decision pipeline |
| **Settings** | Your business profile and governance |

---

## Overview Tab

**Health Score** — A 0–100 score representing overall business performance across sales, operations, finance, customer experience, and technology. Green (70+) means healthy. Yellow (40–69) means needs attention. Red (below 40) means critical action required.

**Key Metrics (KPI Strip)** — The most important quantified signals derived from your business health data, tool execution history, and workflow completions.

**Operating Loop Status** — Three counters showing:
- Active constraints blocking your business
- Recommendations ready to act on
- Items waiting for your approval

**Decision Pipeline** — Every decision the BOSS operating loop has generated, with confidence scores and current status (generated → reviewed → approved → executing → completed).

---

## Timeline Tab

A chronological feed of everything that has happened in your business operating system:

- Business assessments completed
- Health scores calculated
- Constraints detected
- Recommendations generated
- Decisions generated and approved
- Plans created
- Workflows completed
- Outcomes verified
- Learnings recorded

Use the timeline to understand *what happened and when*, trace decisions back to their root causes, and audit every automated action.

---

## Approvals Tab

Everything that needs your review before BOSS acts. Two queues:

**Pending Decisions** — Decisions generated by the operating loop with confidence scores below the auto-approve threshold (< 75%). Review the executive brief, then approve or reject.

**Pending Recommendations** — Strategic recommendations generated from constraint analysis awaiting your endorsement before entering the decision pipeline.

All approvals are logged to the immutable event audit trail.

---

## Automation Tab

**Integrations** — Every external provider (Slack, email, CRM, calendar, payment processor) and whether it is connected. Connect or disconnect integrations here.

**Execution History** — Every tool execution the operating loop or a workflow has performed, with status (succeeded / failed / pending) and timestamp.

---

## Intelligence Tab

**KPI Readings** — Quantified metrics derived from your business health score, workflow completion rate, and tool execution history.

**Active Signals** — Three counters: active constraints (blocking performance), active recommendations (available actions), decisions in the pipeline.

**Decision Pipeline** — The full decision queue with confidence percentages and status badges.

---

## Settings Tab

**Business Profile** — Your business ID, name, type, employee count, and years operating.

**Governance** — How BOSS protects your data:
- Immutable event log (nothing is deleted)
- Multi-tenant isolation (your data never touches another organization's)
- Organizational memory (learnings retained from every verified outcome)

---

## The 15-Minute Value Path

A new customer reaches their first success moment in this order:

1. **Create business** — name, industry, employees (2 min)
2. **Complete Business MRI** — answer 5 diagnostic questions (5 min)
3. **View health score** — see the Overview tab (30 sec)
4. **See first constraint** — understand what is blocking the business (1 min)
5. **Approve first recommendation** — take the first action (2 min)
6. **Watch the timeline** — see the event log fill with evidence (30 sec)
7. **First success moment** — KPIs visible, first approved decision, operating loop queued (4 min)

Total: under 15 minutes from registration to first meaningful action.
