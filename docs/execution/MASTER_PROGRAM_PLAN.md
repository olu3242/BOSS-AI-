# BOSS Master Program Plan

## Authority

Adopted 2026-06-28 as the primary delivery sequence.

| Program | Objective | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 Platform Kernel | Stabilize identity, registries, context, knowledge, CPP, UCR, SDK, governance | In progress; CPP Engineering GO |
| 2 Business Intelligence | Diagnostics, health, gaps | Planned |
| 3 Business Strategy | Reasoning, objectives, constraints | Planned |
| 4 Business Planning | Plans, priorities, resources | Planned |
| 5 Business Execution | Workflow, automation, AI, policies, approvals | Planned |
| 6 Business Optimization | Forecasting, optimization, simulation, recommendations | Planned |
| 7 Executive Operations | Mission Control, dashboard, ledger, operating cycle | Planned |

Every capability includes architecture, foundation, runtime, integration,
validation, certification, and documentation. Certification is evidence-based:
Engineering GO, GO With Environmental Blockers, Production GO, or NO-GO.

Releases progress through Development, Integration, Internal Alpha, Internal
Beta, Production Candidate, Production, and Operational Monitoring. Version
1.0 requires all programs at Engineering GO, production blockers closed,
end-to-end lifecycle validation, and stable operational monitoring.

Changes require dependency and architecture review, certification impact
analysis, and an updated execution roadmap.
