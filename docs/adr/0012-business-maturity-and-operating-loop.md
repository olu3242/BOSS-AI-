# ADR 0012: Adopt Business Maturity and Operating Loop Models

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-06-28

## Context

The Constitution and Canonical Business Model define intent and semantics, but
BOSS also needs a customer evolution roadmap and one continuous operating cycle
to prevent features, agents, and workflows from becoming independent products.

## Decision

Adopt `BUSINESS_MATURITY_MODEL.md` as the evidence-based customer roadmap and
`BUSINESS_OPERATING_LOOP.md` as the canonical continuous operating cycle.
Establish the Architecture Review Board process and embed its questions in pull
request review.

Maturity and loop stages are target contracts, not claims of current
implementation. Promotion and autonomy require executable evidence.

## Consequences

- Product capabilities map to maturity evidence and a loop stage.
- Industry Packs specialize one model rather than creating vertical runtimes.
- Business IQ remains gated until scoring is reproducible and evidence-backed.
- PI-2 remains decomposed and blocked behind UCR prerequisites.
